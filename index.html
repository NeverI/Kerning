Hi! Here is a playground for text kerning.

Click on a letter and press LEFT or RIGHT arrow key and the letter spacing will change.
You also can press UP and DOWN key for vertical nudge. (Tip: WASD keys ;)
Hold your SHIFT button for bigger movement.

Double click on the text and you can overwrite it.
ESC will cancel, ENTER will confirm the changes.
SHITF + ENTER will insert a new row.
